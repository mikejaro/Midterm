Our Stadium!

1996 - Paul G. Allen obtained an option to purchase
the Seattle Seahawks.

He then launched a campaign to win public support
for a new world-class football/soccer
stadium and exhibition center.

2002 - The Seahawks opened the 2002 preseason against
the Indianapolis Colts in new Seahawks Stadium.

2004 - Our stadium is renamed Qwest Field.

2009 - Awarded the “Event Recycling Award”
from the Washington State Recycling Association.

2011 - Given our new name of CenturyLink Field.
